Portfolio Protection Planner

Build a protection plan for your stock portfolio.

This app does not place trades. It reviews your stock holdings, compares hedge approaches, and suggests options to consider based on your protection goal, budget, and time horizon.

What you'll get

  • Portfolio-level protection analysis, not just one stock at a time.
  • A comparison between single-stock puts and an index hedge for diversified portfolios.
  • Clear estimates for cost, protection, scenario outcomes, and review timing.

For education and planning only. Options involve risk and can lose value quickly.
